Genetic Analysis

Our body is controlled by a set of genetic instructions known as our genome or chromosomal blueprint. Our individual DNA provides codes for everything from eye colour to antibody production. It determines our risk for disease, our reacton to stress, our response to illness or injury and how we respond to food, excerise and medication. As an example, some people seem to respond well to high-protein low-carb diets, while for others it is inefficacious. Some individuals benefit from more excerise while others need to control stress. What fits your body best is all coded in your DNA.
